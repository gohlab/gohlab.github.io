THANKS FOR PARTICIPATING IN OUR WORKSHOP!!!

Scientific Workshop on qPCR
​
"A cross-platform user-friendly qPCR data analysis template with publication-ready figure."

This workshop is aimed at giving participants a detailed understanding of real-time quantitative PCR from experimental design & qPCR data analysis to journal publication.

Hands-on training of qPCR data analysis to publication figures / tables.
​
Upon the completion of this one-day online workshop, participants will be capable of the following:
  • Conceptualising a qPCR experiment with different considerations
  • qPCR primer design ​
  • Aware of the different considerations in conducting standard curve and gene expression experiment
  • Understand the different calculations of relative & absolute quantification gene expression
  • Understand the different applications of qPCR
  • Hands-on in qPCR data analysis using an Excel template
  • Network opportunity with other researchers in the field & sharing of knowledge via discussion

Workshop Highlights

  1. Principles of qPCR primer design
  2. Experimental design & considerations
  3. Complete workflow of RT-qPCR analysis
  4. qPCR applications
  5. Technical talks on the latest technological advancement
  6. A MS Excel template for user-friendly  analysis of qPCR data from any instrument
  7. Workshop materials & video recordings will be available for access after the workshop
  8. International participants
  9. Interactive discussion
  10. ​Certificate of participation

Why should YOU attend?

  • You're a researcher, academician, or student interested in qPCR and its applications.
  • You're interested in advanced skills in qPCR data analysis.
  • You want user-friendly platforms for the analysis of qPCR data.
  • You got the qPCR results but not sure how to interpret them.
  • You wish to analyse the qPCR data hands-on & drawing the correct figure with error bars.
  • You work on a gene expression project and want to learn on how to publish your results in a high-impact journal.

​This workshop is opened to local & international participants, from beginner to advanced researchers in molecular biology. The content of this workshop is suited to all levels of learners.
