THANKS FOR PARTICIPATING IN OUR WORKSHOP!!!

Continuous Professional Development (CPD) Workshop
How to GOT & have a fulfilled life?
​
"An insightful workshop on how to survive and thrive in the postgraduate journey."

This workshop is aimed at giving participants a detailed understanding of what to expect while pursuing a postgraduate research degree.

Motivational life skills transferable to everyday life.
​
Upon the completion of this one-day online workshop, participants will be capable of the following:
  • Aware of the requirements of a postgraduate study
  • Understand the different expectations from supervisors
  • Understand the different aspects of a research degree
  • Acquired life skills on the different tools for the management of personal effectiveness, time, project & stress
  • Network opportunity with other postgraduates in the field & sharing of experience via discussion

Workshop Highlights

  1. The essentials of a postgraduate study
  2. Managing supervisor-student relationship
  3. Route to Graduate on Time
  4. Things about Master & PhD that nobody told you
  5. Postgraduate survival skills
  6. Beyond Master/ PhD
  7. Workshop materials & video recordings will be available for access after the workshop
  8. International participants
  9. Interactive discussion
  10. ​Certificate of participation

Why should YOU attend?

  • You're a researcher, academician, or student starting a postgraduate degree.
  • You're interested in pursuing a postgraduate study.
  • You want insights on how to graduate on time (GOT).
  • You want to learn how to balance work and life.
  • You need advice on how to be successful in a postgraduate journey.

​This workshop is opened to local & international participants, from final year undergraduate, masters to PhD candidates and even new postgraduate supervisors. The content of this workshop is suited to all levels of learners.
